Method Overview

Our method leverages a unified 3D Gaussian representation to jointly model motion blur and defocus blur across multi-view images. The key contributions include:

  • Dual-blur perception module for pixel-level blur mask estimation.
  • Blur-aware Gaussian splatting with gradient optimization.
  • UGR-Opacity adaptive optimization for blurred regions.
