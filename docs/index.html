üîç Method Overview

Our method leverages a unified 3D Gaussian representation to jointly model motion blur and defocus blur across multi-view images. The key contributions include:

  • Dual-blur perception module generates pixel-level blur masks to explicitly mitigate motion and defocus blur during 3D reconstruction.
  • Blur-aware Gaussian splatting incorporates blur masks for gradient optimization, ensuring accurate modeling and avoiding degraded information.
  • UGR-Opacity adaptive optimization refines Gaussian representations in under-optimized blurred regions, improving accuracy and quality.
